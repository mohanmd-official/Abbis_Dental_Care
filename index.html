FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer painless root canal treatment?+

Yes, we use modern microscopic and rotary endodontic techniques for comfortable, virtually painless root canal treatments, often completed in a single sitting.

Are aligners available at affordable prices?+

Yes, we offer affordable clear aligner options in Salem along with traditional metal and ceramic braces to suit every budget.

Do you treat children?+

Yes, our pediatric dentistry services include preventive care, habit correction and child-friendly treatment in a comfortable environment.

Do you provide emergency dental care?+

Yes, we provide emergency dental care for urgent issues like severe pain, swelling, or dental trauma.

How long does teeth whitening take?+

Professional teeth whitening at our clinic typically takes about 45–60 minutes for a noticeable, lasting improvement.

Do you accept online appointment booking?+

Yes, you can book an appointment instantly using our online form, available on this website.
